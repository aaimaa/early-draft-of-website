About me

My name is Robin Stickel and I'm a 22 years old designer currently living in Düsseldorf, Germany. Since starting as a freelancer in 2015, I've worked with various clients from a range of industries. I also have experience with traditional advertising and working for agencies. As a Graphic and Web Designer my main goal is to create the best possible experience for as many users as possible. I excel at creating minimal, yet effective visual identities and digital solutions. This includes simple-to-use, human-centered designs as well as intuitive user experience.

While I consider myself a designer first and foremost, I have experience with frontend development, SEO optimization and social media marketing. Constantly learning and experiencing new things. Other than that I'm passionate about photography, traveling, videogames and soccer.
